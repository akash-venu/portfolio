Integrated Analysis of Covid-19

Spearheaded the development and design of stakeholder-centric COVID-19 dashboards leveraging SQL, Tableau, and Python, aimed at bolstering analytical capabilities. These dashboards not only empowered citizens by offering in-depth insights into infection rates, death rates, and vaccination progress across multiple countries but also played a pivotal role in facilitating informed decision-making. By enabling real-time monitoring, the dashboards allowed for swift responses to potential outbreaks and the emergence of new variants, ensuring proactive measures and effective containment strategies.
