Ecommerce Website for Herstellung

I spearheaded the architecture and deployment of a real-time Ecommerce platform for Herstellung, leveraging the flexibility and user-friendly environment of WordPress. In orchestrating this project, I took charge of coordinating with GoDaddy for reliable hosting solutions, integrating WordPress as the core CMS to manage content and site functionalities, and incorporating Instamojo as a secure payment gateway to facilitate seamless transactions. To ensure an enhanced user experience and streamline the shopping journey, I meticulously designed and optimized key webpages including the Home, Contacts, Products, Cart, Checkout, Payment, and Invoice Mail pages. Each design was tailored with a focus on user-centricity, aiming to provide intuitive navigation, clear product presentation, and straightforward checkout processes. By integrating these elements cohesively, I aimed to create an efficient and engaging online shopping environment that aligns with Herstellung's brand values and customer expectations.
