Skull Stripped MRI Quality Evaluation

I worked with a comprehensive dataset comprising 2,060 brain MRI scans, meticulously partitioning it into training and testing sets to ensure a balanced and accurate model evaluation. Harnessing the power of Keras and Tensorflow, I delved into deep learning techniques, developing neural network architectures tailored for medical imaging analysis. To handle the intricate .nii file format, I utilized nibabel to seamlessly extract the required data, ensuring the integrity and accuracy of the information processed. In the pursuit of optimizing our model's performance, I employed Matplotlib for visualizing our results and dataset characteristics. This approach not only facilitated a deeper understanding of the dataset's intricacies but also enabled the crafting of a robust model. By focusing on both data analysis and model refinement, I aimed to enhance the overall performance and reliability of our predictive capabilities.
