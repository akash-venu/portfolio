Exploring New York City Airbnb Data through Analysis and Visualization

I spearheaded the integration of Airbnb and NYPD data, merging two distinct datasets to provide a holistic view of trends, safety, and booking patterns in New York City. Leveraging Tableau's visualization capabilities, I designed interactive dashboards that offered stakeholders intuitive and insightful views into these intertwined datasets. To deepen the analysis, I applied regression analysis to identify underlying trends and sentiment analysis to gauge public perceptions and attitudes towards safety. These analytical methods yielded valuable crime insights, which became instrumental in shaping strategic decisions for both Airbnb hosts and guests. By synthesizing this data-driven intelligence, I empowered users to make informed choices when booking Airbnb accommodations. The insights derived helped users identify safer options, enabling them to navigate the accommodation landscape with heightened awareness and confidence.
