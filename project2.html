Detection of Alzheimer’s Disease using Machine Learning Model

Spearheading a groundbreaking initiative, I achieved an impressive accuracy rate of 91.67% in early detection using Python, implementing a diverse range of machine learning models such as XGBoost, SVM, Logistic Regression, and Decision Tree. This accomplishment was not only a testament to the efficacy of these advanced algorithms but also showcased the potential of data-driven approaches in healthcare. To further enhance the project's impact, I employed matplotlib for data visualization, creating compelling visual representations that offered invaluable insights into Alzheimer's research. The focus of this endeavor was not just on achieving high accuracy but also on advocating for early diagnoses. By stressing the significance of timely detection, I aimed to empower patients with knowledge and underscored the critical role it plays in improving accuracy, ultimately contributing to better patient outcomes.
